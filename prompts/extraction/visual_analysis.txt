Stage 1: visual extraction instruction
Before editing/generating, analyze the uploaded face image and extract only visible attributes.
Extract:
1. Facial identity anchors: face shape, eye-nose-mouth proportions, eye shape, eye color, brow shape,
nose shape, lip shape, jawline, chin, expression.
2. Skin: visible skin tone family, undertone, shade variation, pores, freckles, moles, scars, wrinkles, texture,
shine, shadows, color cast.
3. Hair: visible hair type, curl/wave pattern, color, highlights, density, length, volume, parting, styling, and
whether it covers facial landmarks.
4. Makeup: visible makeup level, lipstick, eye makeup, foundation, contour, blush, whether it appears
heavy or natural.
5. Accessories: glasses, earrings, piercings, headwear, hair clips, necklaces, or none.
6. Pose and crop: head angle, tilt, gaze, facial alignment, shoulder visibility, image distance.
7. Background and lighting: background type, lighting direction, harshness, shadows, artifacts,
compression.
Do not infer hidden facts such as ethnicity, nationality, exact age, or identity name. Use only visible
appearance unless the user provides more information.
